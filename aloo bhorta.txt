Try with these quantity
INGREDIENTS
250 g potatoes
50 g onions
5 g garlic
2 pcs green chillies
2 pcs dried red chillies
4 g salt
8 g mustard oil

Mix it all together and crush to make the bharta. Serve with rice. Best served with fried egg.
