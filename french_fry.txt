1. Wash the potatos properly
2. Peel off the potatos gently
3. Cut the peeled off potatos thinly cubes
4. Wash the sliced potato cubes in cold water for 2/3 times
5. Put the oil on the stove and heat it for 5 mins
6. Put the potatos into the pot and fry for 7/8 mins
7. Take the fries out when they are golden brown.
